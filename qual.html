Auton Info

This is where you will describe the robot's actions during the Autonomous period! (You can scroll on this menu)

General Guidelines

Do your best to accurately describe the robot's path. This is very important for our drive and strategy members, since knowing what a robot does and when can greatly help us cooperate with other teams.

Useful Terms

Outpost: The Outpost is on the corner of the field next to the Human Player. It can both accept and deliver balls to the robots.
Depot: The Depot is a section of the field containing an amount of pre-set balls. Robots must drive over a raised perimeter to access it.
Pre-load: Robots can be pre-loaded with up to eight balls. It's not likely that robots at Champs will only shoot pre-loaded balls.
[Times] Sweep: Sweeping refers to a robot going into the neutral zone and intaking balls. Replace [Times] with how many times it repeats this action.
Sweep x Bump: A robot that sweeps and only goes over the bump.
Sweep x Trench: A robot that sweeps and only goes over the trench.
Trench x Sweep x Bump: A robot that reaches the Neutral Zone using the trench, sweeps, but then elects to go over the Bump instead of through the trench again.
